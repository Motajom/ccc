Holy, Holy, Holy

English

1. Holy, Holy, Holy
Holy, Holy, Holy
Holy, Holy, Holy
Is the Lord divine full of light
Everlasting King
Is the Lord most glorious
King of host of holy Angels
2. Holy, Holy, Holy
Holy, Holy, Holy
Holy, Holy, Holy
Is the song of adoration
That the holy Angels
Sin around the Heavenly throne
Praising the creator of Heaven and Earth.
3. Holy, Holy, Holy
Holy, Holy, Holy
Holy, Holy, Holy
Sing the sun, moon and all the stars
The waves of the sea
And all the people of the earth
All bow down before our creator. Amen
