Hymn 58

Sing ye raise your voices in songs

English

Sing ye raise your voices in songs
Unto the Lord our King
That Holy King might redeem us
In this his Holy fold
That Holy King might redeem us
And send his Heavenly help
To keep us off all sinful deeds
And forgive us our sins.
Amen.
